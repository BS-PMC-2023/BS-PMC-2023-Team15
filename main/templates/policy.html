Privacy Policy for VCStorage

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at info@sce.ac.il.

At VCStorage we consider the privacy of our visitors to be extremely important. This privacy policy document describes in detail the types of personal information is collected and recorded by VCStorage and how we use it.

Eligibility for Borrowing

  • Item Availability
    • Our website or mobile app will indicate the availability of items for borrowing. However, availability may vary based on demand and prior reservations.
  • Reservation
    • You may reserve an item in advance through our website or mobile app. Reservations are subject to item availability and are confirmed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Borrowing Period
    • Each item has a designated borrowing period, which will be communicated during the reservation process. It is your responsibility to return the item within the specified timeframe to avoid late fees or penalties.
  • Item Condition
    • Before borrowing an item, please carefully inspect it for any existing damage or issues. Report any concerns to our staff to ensure accurate documentation and prevent misunderstandings upon return.

    Eligibility for Returns

  • Defective or Damaged Items
    • If you receive a defective or damaged item, please notify us within 2 days of receipt. We will arrange for the return and replacement of the item at no additional cost to you.
  • Incorrect Item
    • If you receive an incorrect item that is different from what you ordered, please contact us within 2 days of receipt. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly by arranging the return and sending you the correct item.
  • Return Deadline
    • The borrowed item must be returned by the agreed-upon date and time. Late returns may incur additional fees or penalties as specified in our terms and conditions.
  • Return Condition
    • You are responsible for returning the item in the same condition as when borrowed, except for normal wear and tear. Any damage or loss beyond normal wear and tear may result in repair or replacement charges.
  • Late Returns
    • If you anticipate a delay in returning the item, please inform us as soon as possible. Late returns without prior notification may affect your borrowing privileges and result in additional fees.
  • Late Fees
    • Late returns may be subject to late fees or penalties as specified in our terms and conditions. These fees are intended to encourage timely returns and ensure fairness for other members.
